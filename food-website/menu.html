Burgers

A burger is a grilled patty and placed between two halves of a bun. Slices of raw onion, lettuce, bacon, mayonnaise, and other ingredients add flavor. Burgers are considered an American food but are popular around the world.

Pasta

Pasta is a very delicious food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking. Add white or red sauce along with some veggies to it.

Beverages

A cold drink is a drink that generally contains water, a sweetener, and a natural and / or artificial flavor. The sweetener can be a sugar, high fructose corn syrup, fruit juice, a sugar substitute (in the case of diet drinks), or a combination of these.
